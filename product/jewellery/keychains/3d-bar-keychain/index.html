Ordering & Process
How do I place an order? +
Select your product, choose quantity and finish, upload your artwork file, and checkout. You can also WhatsApp us or walk in to Paya Lebar Square.
What file format do you accept? +
PDF at 300dpi with 3mm bleed is ideal. We also accept AI, EPS, high-res PNG and JPG. We do a pre-press check on every file before printing.
Do I get a proof before printing? +
Yes — we check your file and flag issues before printing. For colour-critical jobs, a hard proof can be arranged at additional cost.
Can I make changes after ordering? +
Changes can be made before the file goes to print. Once printing starts, changes are not possible. Contact us ASAP if you need to amend.
Turnaround & Delivery
How fast can you print? +
Standard turnaround is 3–5 working days. 24-hour express is available for most products — order before 12pm for same-day processing.
Do you deliver? +
Yes — island-wide delivery at +S$8 flat. Or collect from our Paya Lebar Square outlet Mon–Sat 10am–7pm.
Can I collect same day? +
For express orders placed before 12pm, same-day collection is possible for select products. WhatsApp us to confirm availability.
Pricing & Payment
Are there hidden fees? +
No. The price you see on the price list is what you pay. Delivery is +S$8 if you choose delivery. Express is +30%. That\'s it.
Do you offer corporate accounts? +
Yes — for businesses with regular print needs. Monthly invoicing, volume pricing, and a dedicated account manager. Contact us to set up.
What payment methods do you accept? +
PayNow, bank transfer, credit/debit card, and cash at our outlet. Corporate accounts on monthly invoice.
Can I get a custom quote? +
Yes — WhatsApp or email us for bulk orders, custom sizes, or anything not listed on our price page.
Quality & Printing
How do you ensure print quality? +
Every job goes through pre-press proofing — consistent colour accuracy, material specs, and production process. Every batch is quality-checked.
Why does my print look different from my screen? +
Screens show RGB colour, printers use CMYK. Always set your file to CMYK mode before sending. We flag this during pre-press checks.
What if I'm not happy with the print? +
If the error is on our end, we reprint at no charge. If the issue is with the file you submitted, we\'ll work with you on a solution. Contact us within 3 days of collection.
Still have questions?

WhatsApp us or walk in — we\'re happy to help.
