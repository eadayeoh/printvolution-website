Issue №02 · The Gift Edit

The honest guide to LED photo gifts.

Custom LED photo bases have become one of Singapore's most popular personalised gifts — and for good reason. Unlike framed photos that fade or mugs that chip, a laser-engraved acrylic base with warm LED lighting becomes a permanent fixture in someone's home. Here's everything worth knowing before you order one.

01

Why acrylic beats glass and wood.

We get asked this a lot. Glass engravings look premium but break easily, weigh too much for LED bases, and the laser marks go cloudy over time. Wood engravings absorb the LED glow poorly — the light gets eaten by the grain, so the design barely shows at night.

Premium clear cast acrylic (what we use) is the sweet spot. It's light, durable, and the laser creates frosted marks that catch LED light beautifully. By day, it's a clean etched display. By night, the engraving glows like it's internally lit — because optically, it basically is.

02

What photos work best (and which don't).

Laser engraving converts photos to high-contrast line art. That means photos with clear subject-background separation work brilliantly — a face against a plain wall, a pet against grass, a couple against sky. Messy backgrounds, very dark photos, or group shots where faces are small? They come out muddy.

Our AI does a decent job of cleaning up so-so photos, but it can't invent detail that isn't there. The 3 free regenerations are there for a reason — try different styles if the first one doesn't feel right. If it still looks off, our team will email you before engraving rather than print something you won't love.

03

Warm white vs cool white vs colour-cycling LED.

Warm white (what most of our customers pick) gives a candle-like amber glow — cozy, bedside-table vibes, flattering for skin tones and family photos. Cool white feels more modern and clinical, better for architectural subjects, logos, or geometric art styles. The 7-colour cycling option sounds fun but honestly reads as gimmicky for most gift contexts — skip unless it's for a kid's room.

All our LED bases run on USB-C or battery. No permanent wall installation, no electrician needed. Plug-and-play from the box.

Before you order

Good questions.

What if I don't like any of the AI-generated results?
You get 3 free regenerations per order. If you've used all 3 and still aren't happy, our design team will manually review your photo and adjust the art before engraving — at no extra cost. We'd rather slow down than engrave something you won't love.
Is the LED light bright enough to see during the day?
The engraving is clearly visible during the day on its own — the LED adds night-time magic but isn't required to see the art. Warm white LED is subtle (candle-like glow), cool white is brighter. Both are designed to look good at night, not to light up a room.
How is it powered?
USB-C cable included (works with any phone charger or laptop port). Battery option available at +S$4 — lasts about 8 hours on a single AA charge. No wall installation, no electrician needed.
Can I use a photo with multiple people?
Yes, but the engraving will be more abstract — individual facial features get simplified in laser art. Works great for couple shots or family groups of 3–4. For groups of 5+, we recommend sending us a photo where everyone's face is clearly visible and close together.
Do you do bulk orders for corporate gifts?
Yes — bulk discount kicks in at 5 units. For 20+, we'll assign you an account manager who can handle direct shipping to each recipient with personalised engraving on each. Get in touch for a corporate quote.
What's the difference in size options?
Small (10×15cm) fits a bedside table or desk nicely. Medium (15×20cm, our most popular) works for living room shelves and mantelpieces. Large (20×28cm) is a statement piece — looks best in entryways or as a focal gift. All sizes use the same engraving detail.
